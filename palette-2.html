Priča o osnivanju

Kako sam postao Zagorski Zet

Open gallery 13 photos

I arrived in Croatia knowing no one and speaking no Croatian. The person who changed that — who changed everything — was Davor Kolarić. Davor ran Konoba Ankora in Kustošija. Our family came from Zagorje, and he ran his birtija the only way that counts — your drink before your coat came off, regulars on their stools, strangers leaving as friends. He was my father-in-law, but never once made me feel like a foreigner.

They called him Medo — Bear — and he earned it. Over countless evenings he taught me how a real Zagorac lives: spek, wine, rakija, how to fill a room and send everyone home happy. And how to order a gemišt — not the tourist 50/50. Prst prst. Two fingers. Wine on top, just enough water.

Davor passed away from lung cancer. I miss him every day. If he were here for ZaZet Solutions, two gemišts would be on the counter before I opened my mouth. In our family I was the Zagorski Zet — the Zagorje son-in-law — and ZaZet is the name this company was always going to carry. On every project I try to bring what he brought to everyone who walked into that birtija: make them feel at home, earn their trust, and do it right — not the tourist version.

🕯️ In memory of Davor Kolarić

FAQ

Česta pitanja

Do I need technical knowledge to work with you?

None at all. You describe the problem in plain language — "I spend two hours every Monday doing X" — and I handle everything technical. You just test and approve the result.

How long does a typical project take?

Small automations: 3–5 days. Websites: 1–2 weeks. Larger systems: 2–4 weeks. I scope tightly so there are no surprises. You'll know the timeline before we start.

What if something breaks after the project is done?

Every project includes a two-week support window. After that, fixes are billed hourly — but most things don't break because I build simply and hand you full documentation.

Do you work with businesses outside Croatia?

Yes. Around a third of my clients are outside Croatia. I work remotely without any issues, and I'm comfortable in English, Hebrew, and functional Croatian.

Postoji li minimalna veličina projekta?

Nema minimuma. Ako je problem vrijedan rješavanja, vrijedi ga riješiti kako treba. Neki od mojih najboljih projekata krenuli su kao "Znam da je ovo malo, ali…" razgovori na WhatsAppu.

Zašto vi, a ne agencija?

Radite izravno sa mnom — bez account managera, bez slojeva. Jedan senior inženjer (Shopify, Siemens) gradi rješenje, fiksni rok i cijena, vi posjedujete kod i dokumentaciju.

Zašto ne Zapier ili Make sami?

No-code alati su odlični za jednostavne stvari. Ja gradim prilagođeno rješenje koje točno odgovara vašem poslovanju — bez mjesečnih pretplata, bez ograničenja koraka, s punom dokumentacijom.

Posjedujem li kod i podatke?

Da. Sve što gradim pripada vama — kod, pristupi, dokumentacija. Nema lock-ina. Možete nastaviti s bilo kojim developerom nakon projekta.

Zašto WhatsApp broj nije hrvatski?

What is gemišt?

Gemišt is a Croatian drink — white wine topped with sparkling mineral water, especially in Zagorje and northwest Croatia. Wikipedia ↗

Locals don’t order “half and half.” You hold up two fingers — one vertical (wine), one horizontal (just enough water). That’s prst prst. The ZaZet logo mirrors it: blue on top, gold below — only the word ZaZet fills; SOLUTIONS stays gold.
