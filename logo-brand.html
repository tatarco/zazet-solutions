10 Variants — Liquid Letters Direction
V1 — Canonical

Pure Gemišt

Exact blue + Graševina gold. The 30/70 liquid ratio runs through every letter. White bubbles concentrated in the blue zone. This is the base.

V2 — Night Glow

Lit from Within

Near-black background. The liquid zones glow outward — a halo of blue light from the letters. Like reading a neon sign through a rainy window.

V3 — Glass Wall

Letter Outlines as Glass Rim

The letter strokes become the glass walls. Liquid visible through the transparent letters. White outline = frosted glass rim. Sophisticated, architectural.

V4 — Three Nations

Blue / Gold / Red

Israeli mineral blue on top, Graševina gold in the middle, Croatian wine red at the bottom. Three layers, three identities — all inside one word.

V5 — Light Mode

Ink on Parchment

Business docs, letterhead, invoices. Pastel blue and soft gold — still readable, still beautiful. Dark outline makes it crisp at any resolution.

V6 — Hierarchical

Za at Capital Scale

Big Z carries the liquid. The "aZet" follows at 2/3 scale. Creates visual weight and hierarchy inside the name itself. The "Za" part dominates — Zagreb, Zagorje.

V7 — Copper Rim

Metallic Letter Walls

The letter outlines are copper-gold — like the rim of an engraved bačvica. Liquid inside, precious metal on the edges. Premium, artisanal feel.

V8 — Animated

Living Bubbles

Bubbles rise through the liquid in the letters — slowly, gently. Pure CSS animation. The logo breathes. Works beautifully as a loading state or hero element.

V9 — Brand Navy

Single Color, Official

Brand navy (#1a1a2e) only. For monochrome print, embossed business cards, dark embroidery. The liquid/air zones read as tonal variation, not color contrast.

V10 — Ultra Minimal

Whisper Version

Liquid barely visible — just a hint of color and 3 bubbles. The white letter outline does the work. Works at any size. Most readable of all versions.
