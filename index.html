Who I am

About

📍 Zagreb, Croatia 🇮🇱 Israeli 10 yrs experience Staff Engineer

I'm Gal Tidhar — an Israeli software engineer living in Zagreb, Croatia. Over the past decade I've worked across the stack at companies ranging from Siemens PLM and Shopify/Flexport to early-stage startups like Novu and Sedric.ai.

I operate at staff level: I write code, design systems, shape roadmaps, and grow engineers. My focus is the intersection of platform engineering, developer experience, and full-stack architecture — the work that makes everyone on the team faster, not just one feature.

ZaZet Solutions is how I work with companies who need that kind of leverage — whether it's a focused engagement on a specific system, or an ongoing staff engineer presence embedded in your team.

"ZaZet" — Zagorski Zet — Croatian for "the foreigner who married a local." Two cultures, one engineer, one company. Like a gemišt: two things that shouldn't mix, but taste better together.
